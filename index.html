About Us

Welcome to Tails of Valhalla, where we embark on epic adventures with your furry companions! Inspired by Norse mythology, we are a professional and dedicated team of dog walkers and trainers who are passionate about providing top-notch care and training services in a truly Viking spirit.

At Tails of Valhalla, we believe in helping dogs lead happy, healthy, and well-behaved lives through regular exercise and balanced training techniques rooted in the power of positive reinforcement. Just as the Norse warriors relied on strength, honor, and loyalty, we uphold these values in our interactions with your canine companions.

When it comes to dog walking, our experienced dog walkers are committed to providing invigorating journeys for your furry warriors. We tailor each walk to suit your dog's breed, age, and energy level, whether it's a spirited exploration through lush forests or a brisk trot along rugged coastal paths. Our skilled walkers are trained to handle dogs of all sizes and temperaments, ensuring a safe and thrilling experience for your beloved four-legged adventurers.

In addition to our fearless walks, we offer comprehensive training services to tame the wildest of behaviors and shape your dog into a true Norse champion worthy of entering the halls of Valhalla. Our team of trainers combines balanced training techniques with the power of positive reinforcement. We believe in finding the right balance between rewarding good behavior and providing appropriate corrections when necessary. By utilizing positive reinforcement methods, we motivate and encourage your furry warrior, fostering a strong bond and achieving lasting results. Whether it's mastering basic obedience commands or conquering more complex challenges, our trainers are equipped with the knowledge and skills to guide your dog towards victory.

At Tails of Valhalla, the safety and well-being of your four-legged warriors are of utmost importance to us. Our team is fully insured and trained in pet first aid and CPR, ready to face any unexpected battles that may arise. We conduct thorough consultations to understand your dog's unique needs and tailor our services to ensure a harmonious and prosperous journey. With our personalized approach, we aim to forge a steadfast bond between you, your dog, and the spirit of the Gods, creating an unforgettable and legendary experience.

We offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate your bustling lifestyle, and our services can be customized to fit the specific needs of your canine warriors. Whether you seek valiant daily walks, occasional quests of adventure, or specialized training programs fit for Odin's chosen companions, Tails of Valhalla stands ready to accompany your furry warriors on their epic odyssey.

Choose Tails of Valhalla for professional, reliable, and fearless care for your beloved canine warriors. Together, let us honor their heritage and guide them on a journey that will make them worthy of entering the halls of Valhalla.
